Every CryptoPunk Trait: Rarity, Expected vs Actual

March 2026  ·  Punksierge

The CryptoPunk generator had 87 possible traits to work with. Each one appeared a different number of times across the 10,000 punks — some by design, some by the math of probability playing out in surprising ways. This is the full breakdown: every trait, its actual occurrence, and how that compares to what you'd expect.

How to read this: The "Expected (uniform)" column shows how many times each trait would appear if the generator had weighted all 87 traits equally — about 316 each. The "Actual" column is reality. The ratio tells you how far the generator skewed each one intentionally.
