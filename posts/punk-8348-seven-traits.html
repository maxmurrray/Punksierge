Punk #8348: The Only 7-Trait Punk in Existence

March 2026  ·  Punksierge

Punk #8348 Male · Medium skin
Traits 7 1 of 1 in existence
Top sale 1,100 ETH ~$1.79M · Oct 2022
Status Not for sale

There are 10,000 CryptoPunks. The generator assigned each one a set of traits — hair, accessories, facial features — pulled from a fixed pool. Most punks ended up with two or three. A few loaded up on four or five. Eleven reached six.

And then there is Punk #8348.

He has seven traits. He is the only one. In a collection of 10,000, he is alone at the top of a category that has exactly one member: himself. The generator never produced another like him — and mathematically, it almost certainly never would again even if you ran 10,000 more punks.

What makes him genuinely extraordinary isn't just the count. It's that you can see all seven. Every single one lands visibly on a 24×24 pixel face.

That's rarer than it sounds. Most punks with multiple traits run into overlap — two accessories fighting for the same pixels, one obscuring the other. The generator doesn't guarantee visibility. It just assigns. #8348 somehow escaped that entirely.

The math: why this punk statistically shouldn't exist

If you treat each trait as an independent event — which is a simplification, but a useful one — the probability of any single punk having all seven of these traits at once looks like this:

Top Hat→1.15%(115 of 10,000)
Classic Shades→5.02%(502 of 10,000)
Earring→24.59%(2,459 of 10,000)
Big Beard→1.46%(146 of 10,000)
Cigarette→9.61%(961 of 10,000)
Buck Teeth→0.78%(78 of 10,000)
Mole→6.44%(644 of 10,000)
Multiply all seven probabilities together 1 in 9,995,009,247 Expected punks with all 7: 0.000001 — essentially zero. Yet here we are.

Roughly 1 in 10 billion. In a collection of 10,000, the expected number of punks with this exact trait combination is effectively zero. The generator produced it anyway. That's not luck — that's a statistical anomaly that shouldn't have happened in a collection this size, and never will again.

What he's worth

In October 2022, someone bid 1,100 ETH — roughly $1.79 million — on Punk #8348. The bid was ultimately withdrawn. His last recorded sale was 85 ETH. He is not currently for sale, and his current holder has shown no sign of listing him.

Statistically improbable. Visually packed. Historically important. Punk #8348 is not the flashiest punk in the collection — no alien skin, no Ape type. He's a regular male punk who somehow drew the most complex hand in the game. And unlike most anomalies, you can see exactly why he's different the moment you look at him.

Count the traits. All seven are right there.
